Feasibility

The last
30 minutes.

Most good ideas fail at the operational level. SurplusEasy is built around how kitchens actually close.

The design principle

"Reduce friction and cognitive load at the end of a shift so participation feels like part of closing down, not an extra project."

T-40
Plan Surplus
Staff list what's available
Takes minutes. No guesswork. Confirm quantity and allergens for the night's surplus window.
T-30
Surplus Rush Window
Orders come in, window closes
Short ordering window opens. After it closes, no new orders. Kitchen continues closing down.
T-15
Pack & Pickup
Pack once, pickup once
Packing happens once. Pickup happens once. Night shift ends on time.
